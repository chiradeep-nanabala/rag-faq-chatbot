# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Billing

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 24 hours.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Account Settings under the "Billing" tab. Click "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely via Stripe.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact support@example.com within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund.

**Q: How do I update my billing information?**
A: Navigate to Account Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. Click "Update" next to your current payment method to enter new details.

## Product Features

**Q: What file formats does the app support?**
A: The app supports PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, PNG, JPG, and MP4 files. Maximum file size is 100MB per upload.

**Q: Is there a mobile app available?**
A: Yes! We have iOS and Android apps available. Search for "ExampleApp" in the App Store or Google Play Store to download.

**Q: How many users can I have on my account?**
A: The Starter plan supports 1 user, the Pro plan supports up to 10 users, and the Enterprise plan supports unlimited users. You can add or remove users at any time from the Team Settings page.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: The web application requires an internet connection. However, our mobile apps support limited offline functionality — you can view and edit previously downloaded content offline, and changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export all your data at any time from Account Settings > Data > Export. Data is exported as a ZIP file containing CSV and JSON files. Exports are ready within 24 hours.

## Technical & Security

**Q: How is my data protected?**
A: All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Servers are hosted in AWS data centers in the US and EU.

**Q: Do you offer Single Sign-On (SSO)?**
A: SSO via SAML 2.0 is available on the Enterprise plan. We support integration with Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, and other identity providers.

**Q: What is your uptime SLA?**
A: We guarantee 99.9% uptime for Pro and Enterprise plans. Our status page at status.example.com shows real-time and historical uptime data.

**Q: How do I integrate with third-party tools?**
A: We offer a REST API and webhooks for custom integrations, plus native integrations with Slack, Zapier, Salesforce, and HubSpot. Full API documentation is available at docs.example.com/api.

**Q: Where can I find the API documentation?**
A: Complete API documentation, including endpoints, authentication guides, and code examples, is available at docs.example.com/api. We also provide SDKs for Python, JavaScript, and Ruby.

## Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: You can reach our support team via live chat (bottom-right of any page), email at support@example.com, or by submitting a ticket at help.example.com. Pro and Enterprise customers also get phone support.

**Q: What are your support hours?**
A: Live chat and email support are available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST. Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 priority support. Our help center at help.example.com is available 24/7.

**Q: How long does it take to get a support response?**
A: Starter plan: within 48 hours. Pro plan: within 8 business hours. Enterprise plan: within 1 hour (24/7).
